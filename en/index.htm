KolibriOS is a remarkably compact yet lightning-fast operating system designed with your needs in mind. It takes up just a few megabytes of disk space and only 12 MB of RAM, meaning you get more speed and efficiency for everyday tasks. Enjoy a complete suite of built-in apps - like a word processor, image viewer, graphical editor, web browser, and over 30 fun games - so you can work, create, and play without any hassle.

Have you ever dreamed about a system that’s ready to go in just a few seconds from power-on, letting you jump straight into what matters without waiting? That's exactly what KolibriOS does! Every app opens instantly when you click, delivering a smooth, frustration-free experience that lets you focus on your projects and leisure. Experience how KolibriOS’s quick start-up and responsiveness can transform your daily computing, making it a refreshing alternative to more cumbersome systems.

Born from its MenuetOS heritage in 2004 and refined through independent development, KolibriOS is now fully open-source, with most of our code released under GPLv2 license. Your invaluable feedback and contributions through help are greatly appreciated. Join a community where your insights help shape a system built for real users like you.

We hope you will enjoy it!
